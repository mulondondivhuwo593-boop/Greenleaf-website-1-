GreenLeaf Organic Store

GreenLeaf is a locally owned Cape Town business specializing in organic fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and eco-friendly household goods. Established in 2018, it’s known for quality, sustainability, and community involvement. The mission is to promote healthy living with fresh, chemical-free products and to raise awareness about sustainable practices. Key audiences include families, young professionals, and people pursuing healthier lifestyles.
